The inaugural Loco Pro card

This card is built to rattle the block

Battle for Main Street is not just another weeknight card. It is the very first Loco Pro Wrestling event, a full-volume downtown throwdown with faction pride, hometown bragging rights, and the kind of crowd noise that bounces off brick all the way down the street.

Expect swagger from The Rising, brute force from Pillars of Mayhem, and enough live-wire energy to make the Opera House feel a lot more dangerous than it looks from the sidewalk.

Need to know

Fast answers before the doors open

What kind of event is this?

A live Loco Pro Wrestling event with a downtown crowd, faction drama, and a poster-worthy atmosphere built for a one-night takeover.

How much are tickets?

General admission is ten bucks. Use the ticket section to email or call ahead if you want to lock in the plan now.

Where is it happening?

Dickens Opera House on Main Street in Longmont. The whole page is built around that downtown, brick-and-neon mood because the location is part of the draw.

When should I show up?

Doors open at 6:30 PM and the bell rings at 7:00 PM. If you want the room buzzing before the first entrance, do not walk in at the last minute.
